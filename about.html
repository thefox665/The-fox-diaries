About Me

The fox behind the diary

Hello, I’m Ina — a Romanian girl living her Seoul era, one soft day at a time. In 2025 I packed my life, my dreams, and my favourite perfumes, and moved across the world to study Korean language and see who I could become here.

I live in Itaewon, drink way too many seasonal coffees, and spend my days balancing language school, part-time work, small adventures, and all the emotional chaos that comes with being a fox-hearted girl in a city full of stories.

Fox Diaries is the place where I collect the moments I don’t want to forget — the calm ones, the chaotic ones, the romantic ones, and the ones that remind me I'm still growing. It’s my Seoul journal, my reflection space, my soft corner of the internet.

If you’re reading this, welcome to my little world. I’m glad you’re here.
